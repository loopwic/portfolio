## Design Context

### Users
Potential collaborators, hiring managers, and technical peers who want to quickly understand Loopwic's front-end engineering taste, interaction craft, and project experience.

### Brand Personality
Experimental, precise, systems-minded. The interface should feel like a living interaction lab: bold enough to be memorable, but grounded enough to signal maintainable engineering.

### Aesthetic Direction
An experimental scrollytelling portfolio with strong black/white/gray contrast and restrained temperature shifts. Motion should connect sections through pinned transitions, spatial transforms, and direct manipulation rather than decorative one-off effects.

### Design Principles
- Use motion as structure: scroll, hover, and pointer movement should explain hierarchy and progression.
- Keep the palette disciplined: warm or cool monochrome with rare accent heat, never rainbow decoration.
- Make the first viewport unmistakably personal and spatial with a real 3D scene.
- Favor asymmetry, scale shifts, and editorial tension over card-heavy layouts.
- Preserve engineering clarity: dramatic surfaces must still be readable, responsive, and maintainable.
