- Derivative SHA-256: `9503dba389954f5120fae9dab2e23d3454bc6d2fc08e59b1b8e8fd611cf86e91`
- Shape reference: [Cherry MX Switches Keyboard Keys previews](https://www.cgtrader.com/3d-models/electronics/computer/cherry-mx-switches-keyboard-keys-3f0615ef-979d-4989-be5d-6f2b03827173)

The immutable source is a byte-for-byte copy of the downloaded GLB. The web
derivative isolates the source model's assembled medium-detail switch. The
plastic lamp and the two positive-Y LED lead clusters are removed, while the
two negative-Y metal clusters are retained as `Mid Contacts`; these are the MX
switch's own contact leaves and two PCB pins. On `Mid Top`, the large
front-facing LED recess is replaced by one flat panel. The smaller horizontal
slot below it has no floor and passes downward into the switch, while the
opposite housing side remains closed. The source's small rectangular and
semicircular LED reliefs are filled against their original boundary vertices,
flush with the roof rather than covered by overlapping geometry. Authored faces
use a matching flat-black material without the source UV normal map. `Mid
Switch`, `Mid Bottom`, `Mid Spring` and their source materials remain unchanged
in the Blender authoring file. The CGTrader page was used only as a visual
proportion reference; no geometry, textures or other asset data from that
listing are included. The website loads this derivative directly and does not
substitute the earlier hand-built geometry.
